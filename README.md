# Eco Picks

**Eco Picks** is a Minecraft mod where you craft leaf pickaxes, combine them into an OP Eco Pickaxe, and befriend Leafee that helps you through the game.

- **Leaf Pickaxes**: Craft pickaxes from different wood types (Oak, Birch, Spruce, Acacia, Dark Oak, Jungle, Cherry, Mangrove, and Pale Oak)
- **Eco Pickaxe**: Combine up to 9 different leaf pickaxes to create an overpowered mining tool with unique abilities
- **Leafee Companion**: A friendly pet mob that:
  - Follows and defends you in battle
  - Stores items in its inventory
  - Converts amethyst shards into Leafee shards to fix/upgrade your pickaxe
  - Can be tamed and commanded
